HOME

LM Phone Store, founded in 2006, was founded by Lara Ichli and Miguel Sadorra. It is a company that aims to provide the best quality products to its customers. It offers the most recently released smartphones in the market and is known for its exceptional post sales services. The company also offers great deals on older models of smartphones.


Since its start in 2006, the company has built multiple branches around the Netherlands. It has branches in Amsterdam, Utrecht, Hoofddorp, and Arnhem.
